What is CineTools?

CineTools is a collection of web apps to help you grade your footage, create LUTs and much more! It is created and maintained by Gerben Schmidt from Filmkit.net

FilmGrade

Which video types does the FilmGrade app support?

Support for video-types largely depends on your browser, but most browsers support mp4 files with the H.264 codec. Chrome also supports playback of mp4 files with the h.265 codec, but the export currently takes up too much memory.

I can't upload or export a certain video

The FilmGrade app uses technology that is pretty new. This means that it might not work in older browsers. Check Can I Use for the current state of browser support.
